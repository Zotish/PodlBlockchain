Funding, Revenue, and LQD Coin

Is it production ready for public launch?
- No. See 15_security_production_readiness.txt for the concrete reasons.

How to approach VC funding (pragmatic path)
- You will not get large VC funding "fast" without proof. The fastest route is to build traction and de-risk the tech.
- Minimum credible package:
  - Public testnet with metrics (uptime, TPS benchmarks, security tests).
  - Clear token model (supply, emissions, validator economics, treasury).
  - Security review or independent audit plan.
  - A pitch deck and 1-pager explaining PoDL and differentiation.
- Build a short milestone roadmap (3-6 months) and show delivery pace.

How to earn revenue from this blockchain
- Transaction fees: base fee + priority fee.
- Validator and LP participation: portion of fees/rewards can flow into a treasury.
- Bridge fees: charge a fee for lock/mint/burn/release flows.
- Infrastructure revenue: hosted nodes, API access, analytics.
- Ecosystem revenue: grant programs + ecosystem tokens or DAO treasury growth.

LQD as the native coin
- In your code, the native balance is in the chain state (not a contract).
- You can brand that native unit as LQD and set the monetary policy in the chain code.
- The sample contract LQDToken is a plugin and is not the native coin.

Suggested next steps to formalize LQD
- Define total supply and emission schedule.
- Define reward splits and treasury flows.
- Decide whether gas fees are burned, allocated to treasury, or paid to validators/LPs.
- Publish a tokenomics spec and integrate it into the reward logic.
