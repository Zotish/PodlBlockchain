Blocks and Rewards

Block structure
- BlockNumber, PreviousHash, CurrentHash, Timestamp.
- Transactions list.
- Gas metrics: GasLimit, GasUsed, BaseFee.
- RewardBreakdown stores detailed split of rewards.

Gas limit adjustment
- GasLimit moves up/down based on previous block usage.
- Bounded by MinGasLimit and MaxGasLimit.

Mining flow (MineNewBlock)
- Builds a new block with dynamic gas limit and base fee.
- Parallel transaction verification.
- Contract calls executed through ContractEngine.
- If tx type is system/LP and not contract_call/create, it can be forced to success.
- Applies balance changes and appends txs.
- Calculates rewards and appends a reward transaction.
- Saves block to DB.

Reward model (CalculateBlockRewards)
- Fixed reward: 200 LQD (configured in InitLiquiditySystem).
- Gas rewards: fee-based with multiplier.
- Split of fixed+gas rewards:
  - 40% to winning validator
  - 20% to participants
  - 35% to LPs (curve-weighted)
  - 5% to long-lock LPs (365-2000 days)
- Participant share splits into:
  - 18% to non-winning validators (curve-weighted)
  - 2% to tx participants (sqrt of tx value)

Notes
- Reward breakdown is stored in block and also credited to account balances.
