Liquidity System (PoDL)

Core idea
- Liquidity Providers (LPs) lock tokens for a duration and gain Liquidity Power.
- Liquidity Power influences validator selection and reward distribution.

Key structures
- LiquidityProvider: address, stake amount, lock time, lock days, total rewards, pending rewards, and unstake flow state.
- LiquidityLocks: per-address lock records (amount + unlock time).
- PoolLiquidity: tracked per pool (smart contract address).

Locking/unlocking
- LockLiquidity(): removes tokens from liquid balance and records lock.
- UnlockLiquidity()/UnlockAvailable(): releases matured locks back to balance.
- StartUnstake(): initiates unstake (does not release instantly).
- ProcessUnstakeReleases(): releases 1% per day after unstake begins.

Liquidity pools and routing
- Pools are keyed by contract address.
- RebalancePoolsEqual() equalizes liquidity across pools.
- Unallocated liquidity is moved into the richest pool first, then rebalanced.

Rewards to LPs
- Block reward split allocates a large portion to liquidity providers.
- A curve-weighted (sqrt) distribution is used.
- There is an extra bonus share for long-lock LPs (365-2000 days).

Notes
- This liquidity model is central to PoDL and is a core differentiator vs pure PoS.
